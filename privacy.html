Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

BLOOM with Quadia ("BLOOM with Quadia," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit bloomwithquadia.com (the "Site"), sign up for our emails, or purchase one of our products, and how we use and protect it.

By using the Site or providing us your information, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you give us directly and a limited amount of information automatically:

  • Information you provide: your first name and email address when you join the Choose You, Sis reset or any of our email lists, and any details you include when you contact us.
  • Purchase information: when you buy a paid product (such as Rest Well, Sis, BLOOM, Sis, or Rose Era Coaching), your payment is processed by a third-party payment provider. We receive confirmation of your purchase and your contact details, but we do not store your full card number.
  • Automatically collected: basic technical and usage data such as browser type, device, and pages visited, which may be gathered through cookies or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the free reset and any products you sign up for or purchase.
  • To send you emails, resources, offers, and updates you have opted into.
  • To process payments and provide customer support.
  • To improve the Site, our content, and our offerings.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Email Marketing

We use MailerLite to manage our email list and send messages. When you subscribe, your name and email are stored by MailerLite on our behalf. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can opt out at any time. You can review MailerLite's own privacy practices on their website.

4. Payments

Paid products are handled by a secure third-party payment processor. Your payment details are entered directly with that processor and are subject to their privacy policy and security standards. We never see or store your full payment card information.

5. Cookies & Analytics

The Site may use cookies and similar technologies to keep the Site working properly and to understand how visitors use it. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended if you do.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us run our business — such as our email platform and payment processor — and only as needed to provide their services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

7. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as your account or subscription is active, or as needed to provide our services and meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. You may ask us to delete your information at any time.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain uses. To make a request, email us at quadia@bloomwithquadia.com.

9. Children's Privacy

The Site and our products are intended for adults and are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach us at quadia@bloomwithquadia.com.
